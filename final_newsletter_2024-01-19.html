Taylor Swift’s ‘1989 (Taylor’s Version)’ Surpasses 2 Million in U.S. Sales

Taylor Swift is killing the game once again, with her ninth album 1989 (Taylor's Version) reaching 2 million sales in the US. The album sold 17,000 copies in just one week, bringing the total to 2.014 million since its release in October. This girl just keeps breaking records and dominating the charts. And let's not forget, she's the only artist to have two albums sell over a million in 2023. Can we say queen of music? Her continued success is no surprise, considering she's been the top-selling artist for the past five years despite the rise of streaming. And she knows how to cater to her super fans by releasing different versions of her albums, including cassette tapes and vinyl records. Plus, have you seen how many of her albums are currently in the top 10? Let's just say she's tied her own record for most albums in the top 10 by a single artist. Girl power at its finest
